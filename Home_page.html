Why Roof Masters

We specialize in sloped and flat roofs serving Homeowners Associations.

Roof Masters, a subsidiary of Build Masters (CGC1505687), is a full-service General and Roofing Contractor with over 20 years of experience in the South Florida construction industry. Based in Boca Raton, Florida, Roof Masters serves both Palm Beach and Broward counties, specializing in roofing and restoration services. As a Florida-certified roofing contractor (CCC1327296), we bring high expertise to every project.

We are proud members of the Better Business Bureau, American Institute of Architects, Institute of Inspection, Cleaning, and Restoration, Roofing Contractors Association of South Florida, and LEED Green Associate. Our commitment to quality is unwavering, with all work adhering to local and national building codes, OSHA regulations, and EPA guidelines.

Roof Masters provides various services, including new construction, reroofs, and repairs. Our highly skilled team oversees every phase of the process, ensuring professionalism, excellence, and customer satisfaction in all our projects.
