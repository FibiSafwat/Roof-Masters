About-us

Roof Masters is a subsidiary of Build Masters, LC

Build Masters, LC is a full-service General Contractor, Roofing, Plumbing and Restoration Contractors with over 100 years combined experience, specializing in clean up and reconstruction following all types of natural and man-made disasters. 

Build Masters, LC is fully licensed, insured, certified, and capable of being bonded. We are a member of the Better Business Bureau, American Institute of Architects, Institute of inspection cleaning and restoration, Roofing Contractors Association of South Florida and LEED Green Associate. 

We operate our business on solid ethics and morals.

We follow all local and national building codes, OSHA regulations, and EPA guidelines that govern our work. We proudly service the tri-county area and our services include REO renovation and repairs, Tenant Improvements, Restoration, Plumbing, and Roofing services. Our scope of work includes new construction, remodeling, and tenant fit-up.

We have the knowledge and manpower to take you through the entire process, from water extraction and mold remediation to full restoration and reconstruction. It takes years to establish a home or a business and just minutes for a fire, flood or hurricane to destroy it. 

At Build Masters, we understand that in times like these, you need someone you can trust. Rest assured you are in safe hands with us. Let us show you how Build Masters, LC can help.

Build Masters, LC is a Full-service Contractor, specializes in all phases of construction. Whether it’s simple water mitigation, remodel, a major renovation, or new construction, we guarantee a smooth construction process so that your project gets completed on time, within budget, and with superior craftsmanship.

Our professional team that you can depend on will carry the ball from start to finish, providing turn-key services and complete peace of mind ensuring smooth construction process and schedule, making sure that we deliver quality finish in a professional and timely manner that meets the satisfaction of all parties.

Our scope of work includes:

  • New Construction
  • Tenant Improvements
  • Remodeling & rehabilitation
  • Restoration & Remediation
  • Plumbing services
  • Roofing services
  • REO rehab services
  • Insurance claims.
  • Design-build services
