Roof re pointing is an essential part of the re-roofing Broward and Palm Beach Counties process and should be done with flexible pointing material. When done correctly you can have a far better finish with flexible pointing material than sand and cement. Basically, re-pointing is what you can see on the side of ridge caps, which is used to seal various angles on your roof. It seals your roof at the join which blocks water and dirt from entering your home. It is the process which involves removal of old and damaged cement work and the fitting of new roof pointing.
